0. Resilience Backbone: Segmented Tri-Leg Thermal Topology
To achieve an absolute, zero-point failure rating, the base implements a Segmented Tri-Leg (Tri-Web) Topology. This combines the efficiency of active centralized networks with the survivability of redundancy, without mass penalties of idle backups.

Architectural Blueprint Specifications
1. Three Active Geographic Arteries (No Idle Lines) 
Arteries 1 (West), 2 (Center), and 3 (East) routed along completely separate geographic paths. 
- Normal operations: Each carries exactly 33.3% of the total thermal load. 
- Failproof Rule: Each artery sized to carry 50% of maximum global peak flow. If one fails, the remaining two automatically scale up for 100% capacity with zero downtime. Eliminates idle line freezing risk.

2. Cross-Tie Interconnect Matrix 
Underground cross-tie lines every 200 meters with fast-acting pneumatic isolation valves (FAIVs). 
- Localized damage (e.g., micrometeorite) triggers instant valve closure. 
- Fluid shunts through cross-ties, bypassing the damaged section while keeping the rest of the artery functional.

3. Localized Phase-Change Material (PCM) Thermal Batteries 
Every biological module, habitat pod, and critical system wrapped in Lithium-Nitrate Trihydrate (LiNO₃ ⋅ 3H₂O) PCM matrix. 
- Passive protection: Activates at 303 K (30°C) phase change. 
- Maintains survivable temperatures (≥ 290 K) for 72 hours during total disconnection. Provides window for automated repairs.

Failure Response Matrix (Integrated Across All Legs)
- Artery 1 localized puncture: Piezoelectric sensors + FAIVs (45ms isolation) → 100% operational via rerouting. 
- Major cave-in on Center Artery: Isolate artery → West/East scale to 50% each → 100% operational (geographic separation prevents cascading damage). 
- Total power blackout: Node-level battery emergency pumps + PCM → Core life support retained. 
- All arteries severed: Pod isolation + PCM passive mode → 72-Hour Survival State for repair by Leg 5 assets.

Integration with Symbiotic Legs: Arteries parallel Prufrock/Moonworm tunnels. PCM protects all bio-zones. Waste-heat supports pyrolysis.
