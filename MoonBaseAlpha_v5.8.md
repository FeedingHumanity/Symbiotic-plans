**Leg 1: Food (Core Symbiotic Module) – Enhanced v5.8 with Biochar**  
**Purpose:** Establish a self-reinforcing biological production, preservation, and storage system that transforms lunar regolith into functional soil while delivering layered, redundant food security with nutritional completeness, sensory variety, and psychological benefits.  

**First-Principles Foundation (Retained + Extended):** Lunar regolith is mineral-rich but lacks organics and bioavailable nitrogen, with extreme radiation, thermal swings, and vacuum challenges. Protected buried habitats combined with biological succession (pioneer species + microbes + macrofauna + Moonworms) enable progressive soil formation. **Biochar Integration:** Controlled pyrolysis units convert biomass wastes into biochar, mixed into regolith beds at optimized ratios (5-20%, monitored by Grok) to create stable porous structure, enhance cation exchange capacity, retain water/nutrients in 1/6g environments, support microbial colonization, and sequester carbon. This addresses regolith's abrasiveness, low water-holding, and toxicity.
